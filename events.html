RANT-n-RAVE

Rise Up! Get Down!

    Rant-n-Rave is meant to inspire individual action and empower community leaders.

    We will have short speeches by community members with a focus on realistic skills and actionable advice.

    In between speakers, there will be musical breaks to chat amongst yourselves or dance off the heavy topics.

    We're also trying to have some special performances to help lighten the mood.

    This month's theme: "How to prepare for a storm - what to do when the power fails".


    This time we will be doing something we've never done before in years of hosting community events: asking for money.

    If you would like to contribute, you can give cash or donate to our real-deal business account via Venmo.

    Donations are not required to attend. We get it; shit's tight right now.
